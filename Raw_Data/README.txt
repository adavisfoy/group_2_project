All data downloaded from https://www.spc.noaa.gov/wcm/#data

Date range 1950-01-01 to 2020-12-31

Downloaded as three seperate files: Tornadoes, Wind, and Hail


* See SPC_severe_database_description.pdf for column descriptions

* See US_FIPS_Codes.xlsx for state and county FIPS codes in USA

Tornadoes
    Damage rating scale is the F-scale (1950-2006) or EF-Scale (2007-present).

Wind
    Convective/thunderstorm related winds only (does not include hurricane or non-thunderstorm damage).  
    Wind data includes both measured/estimated wind gusts and wind damage without estimated wind speeds (a default wind query with a min speed of 0 includes all such reports).  
    Speed is in knots (50 kt/58 mph and greater is considered severe).

Hail
    Size is inches in diameter (0.75 inch or larger is severe).
